16 March 2020

  • I am currently at Shakeys Mandaluyong
    • Manila in total lockdown aka Enhanced Community Quaurantine
      • Booked my flight to come here prior to the lockdown, and the news keeps getting worse.
      • There is an 8pm curphew and very strict rules that need to be followed.
      • Heard that a total lockdown will soon take effect meaning public transportation won't operate and businesses will need to close down.

Today in the morning we were called by reception at the hotel I was staying in Guadalupe that they are closing their hotel and I needed to be transferred. A bit annoying but what can you do? There's a lockdown and all businesses are shutting their doors. They transferred us to ZEN Rooms in Mandaluyong. It was a nice upgrade and a good view of the city. But there was no Wi-Fi and I needed to study, and Kath needed to work! So we headed to Shakeys down the road and spent the next 5 days there punching away at our keyboards whilst clearing their menu one by one.
