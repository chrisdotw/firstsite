18 March 2020

  • Last day in the Philippines and even more crazy news.
    1. Whilst staying in Michaels condo, new reports stated that our barangay was in "extreme enhanced community quarantine" which meant no one in or out.
    2. Packed our stuff and decided to head to Marikina just before that took effect.

So have you noticed the way I've been typing up my blogs? I know it's pretty boring but as each day progresses my style of writing slightly changes. And part of that is because I'm adding more, or updating previous days, and I am learning new coding techniques. So you notice theres a breakline in between this and the dot points above? Yeah, I wrote those dot points on that specific day but I am updating this part on the 19th March 2020, on an airplane bound to Singapore.

I am keeping it pretty simple, I might add photos here and there once I learn how to. I'm currently only using breaklines and paragraphs as that's all I know pretty much. Anyway let's talk about this day.

So the condo I stayed in is just a small studio room. There were 5 of us sleeping in the room. Pretty cramped right? And the lockdown got us doing nothing. Nothing at all. In fact I couldn't study properly due to the fact that there wasn't enough space and my back would start aching and *hiney* would ache due to the chair being so hard.

Anyway, I went for a peaceful walk down E Rod Ave. About 30 minutes, or 2.5KM walk. Shortly after returning back to the condo I studied for an hour before falling asleep.

I woke up to the sound of Sunshine and Cat talking about how they have upgraded the lockdown to an "extreme enhanced community quarantine", whatever that means. Anyway I had my doubts and did some fact checking on a few websites to find out that other reputable sources (Rappler, GMA, Manila Bulletin) had confirmed that this was the case and the lockdown meant no one in or out of a particular area. That area being the 200m stretch of road that the condo was conveniently located.

I tried booking other hotels online but to no avail. Eventually we decided to just head to Marikina and stay the night at Sunshine's grandfathers house. It had Wi-Fi and space to work in private. Sweet.

There is not much I have learnt today except just the basic breaklines and paragraphs. I learnt how to put photos on and link other web pages but since I don't have internet at the moment I am unable to continue studying.
